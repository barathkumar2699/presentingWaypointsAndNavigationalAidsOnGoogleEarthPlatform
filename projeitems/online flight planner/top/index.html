Site description
The purpose of this site is to quickly create a flight plan for FS 2004, FS X, X-plane, FlightGear or other add-ons with estimated fuel quantity calculation. The flight plan based on calculations by http://rfinder.asalink.net/free and the fuel plan based on calculations by http://fuelplanner.com. The output of these sites will be converted to many downloadable file formats.

Cookies must be enabled for proper working!
